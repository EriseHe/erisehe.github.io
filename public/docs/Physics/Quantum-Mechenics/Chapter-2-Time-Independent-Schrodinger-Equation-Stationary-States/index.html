    Furthermore, expectation value of dynamical variables are also time independent $$ \langle Q(x,p)\rangle=\int , dx ,\Psi^* (x,t) \dots $$ $$ \boxed{\hat{H}\Psi(x)=E\Psi(s)} $$ $E$ is the eigentvalue here Stationary states are states of definite energy: $$ \hat{H}=-\frac{\hbar}{2m}\frac{d^{2}}{dx^{2}}+V(x) $$ This is an example of an eigenvalue equation of the operator $H$.
